# **App Name**: Wonderful Food Supplements

## Core Features:

- Product Catalog & Detail Pages: Display health products across various categories with comprehensive details, including high-quality images, pricing in Kenyan Shillings, benefits, and ingredients.
- Interactive Shopping Cart: Enable users to seamlessly add, remove, and adjust quantities of products in their cart, culminating in a clear order summary before checkout.
- User Accounts & Profiles: Implement secure user registration and login, providing personalized dashboards for customers to manage their orders, delivery addresses, and view purchase history.
- Multi-Payment Checkout: A secure and efficient checkout process with integrated support for local M-Pesa payments and international Debit/Credit Cards.
- Administrative Product & Order Management: A streamlined web-based administration panel for the business owner to effortlessly add new products, edit existing ones, organize product categories, and monitor incoming customer orders.
- AI-Enhanced Product Content Tool: An intuitive tool within the administration panel that leverages generative AI to assist in crafting compelling product descriptions and highlighting key health benefits based on product information.
- Customer Engagement Features: Implement a wishlist functionality allowing users to save preferred products, alongside the integration of customer review displays on product pages to build trust and social proof.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: A calming and sophisticated muted green (#3E7A45) to evoke health, nature, and reliability. Chosen for its balance of vibrancy and professionalism, suitable for conveying wellness without being overtly loud. It will provide excellent contrast on lighter backgrounds.
- Background Color: A subtle, desaturated sage tint (#EEF7EF) that provides a fresh and clean canvas. This choice maintains continuity with the primary hue while ensuring content remains paramount and highly readable within a light aesthetic.
- Accent Color: A fresh, lively lime green (#8ECC4C) serves as an energetic accent. This color is strategically analogous to the primary hue, ensuring harmony, while its higher brightness and saturation create a noticeable pop for interactive elements and calls to action.
- Headline and Body Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif). Chosen for its contemporary, highly readable, and neutral characteristics, perfectly suiting a modern, professional health e-commerce platform. Its versatility ensures clarity for both concise headlines and extensive product descriptions.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, minimalist outline icons throughout the site. These will convey functionality clearly without clutter, reinforcing the modern and professional aesthetic across navigation, product features, and user account elements.
- Implement a highly responsive, mobile-first grid layout that ensures optimal content presentation across all devices. Employ ample whitespace and clear typographic hierarchy to enhance readability and ease of navigation, promoting a streamlined user experience.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful micro-interactions such as smooth fades for content loading, gentle scaling effects on interactive product cards or buttons, and fluid transitions for cart updates. These will provide a premium feel without distracting from the user journey.
